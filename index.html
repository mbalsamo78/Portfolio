My name is Marco Balsamo! Welcome to my portfolio. I am a graduate of Oakland University, having studied History and Social Studies. I am proud to say that I have a passion for teaching and love working with kids. My goal is to shape the future for the next generation of young people!

Education Philosophy

As a philosophy, my focus in the classroom is ensuring that all of my students are engaged in a positive way. I want my classroom to be a place where all children can look forward to being. With that said, I do intend to follow the curriculum as provided by the State, which can be challenging. Determination and a willingness to learn is essential in my classes. School is a place where students should feel safe and welcomed, and my class is no different. My goal is to provide children with a unique learning experience and creating lasting memories for them to take with them for the rest of their lives. Students who take my classes will also learn life skills such as organization, career readiness, and how to be productive members of society. Ultimately, my goal is to provide young people with the tools they need to be successful in the real world!
